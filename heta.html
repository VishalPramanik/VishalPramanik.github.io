Hessian-Enhanced Token Attribution (HETA)

This page embeds the live Gradio demo of our NeurIPS 2025 FoRLM work on Hessian-Enhanced Token Attribution (HETA). You can type your own input, see the model’s generation, and inspect token-level attributions.

The interactive demo is running on a separate backend (Colab + Gradio). Your inputs are sent only to that demo, and the underlying HETA implementation remains private.

If the embedded view doesn’t load, you can also open it directly: Open HETA Demo in a new tab .
